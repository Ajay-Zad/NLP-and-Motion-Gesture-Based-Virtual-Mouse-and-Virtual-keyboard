Blind Keyboard

Specialized keyboards available that can speak out every letter typed by the user. These keyboards are designed to provide auditory feedback, enabling users to hear the characters they are typing.They typically use a built-in text-to-speech engine or connect to screen reader software on the computer or mobile device. When a key is pressed, the associated letter or character is spoken aloud, allowing the user to audibly confirm what has been typed.
